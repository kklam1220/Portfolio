Original Cantonese Play “The Clowns” Additional Performance
Thanks to your incredible enthusiasm for "The Clowns," we're thrilled to announce an additional performance on Saturday, July 5th at 3 PM!

Synopsis:  With his wedding just around the corner, Kwok Ho encounters an  uninvited guest. After a long separation, Siu Yan brings him an  unexpected surprise. Faced with the past, where will the two of them go?  This is a story about letting go, or the inability to let go.

"The Clown" is the winning script of the 9th "Heart River Cultivation Programme".

Playwright：KK Lam

Director：Tiffany Lai

Actors：Tiffany Lai, Dennis Chan

This play contains mature content. Admission is restricted to individuals aged sixteen or above only.
